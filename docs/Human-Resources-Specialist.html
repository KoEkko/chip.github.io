    HR Specialist
    Singapore 2022.10.31

    We are looking for a Human Resources Specialist to be part of our team. The incumbent will work closely with the HR Team on day-to-day HR and Recruitment activities.

    Key Responsibilities:

    ●    Perform full scope of recruitment activities from job posting to screening to onboarding & offboarding

    ●    Participate in career fairs and internship programs

    ●    Prepare HR letters such as letter of appointment, confirmation, and termination etc

    ●    Drive HR best practices and programs to meet business objectives

    ●    Maintain personal records and documents

    ●    Handle employee general queries on leave, claims etc

    ●    Handle work pass matters

    ●    Support the implementation of employee engagement and welfare activities

    ●    Support in HR activities and projects/initiatives

    Job Requirements:

    ●    Preferably Diploma/Degree in Human Resource Management/Business Administration or equivalent

    ●    Minimum 2 years of relevant working experience

    ●    Prior experience in the Wholesale Trade/Electronics industry is an advantage

    ●    Proficient in Microsoft Office

    ●    Familiar with Employment Act and Labor Law

    ●    Strong interpersonal skills and a team player

    Why Join Us:

    ●    Central working location

    ●    5 day's work week

    ●    Medical benefits

    ●    Birthday leave

    ●    Attractive compensation package

    ●    Career development


    We regret that only shortlisted candidates will be notified.
