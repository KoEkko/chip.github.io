    Sourcing Specialist
    Singapore 2022.10.31

    If you are a proactive and strategic sourcing professional with a passion for driving supplier excellence on a global scale, we encourage you to apply.

    Job Responsibilities

    ●    Responsible for managing end-to-end procurement and fulfilment processes, supplier development, and global supplier partnerships

    ●    Develop and execute strategies for identifying and developing semiconductor suppliers on a global scale to expand and diversify the supplier base

    ●    Monitor and evaluate geopolitical, regulatory, and market trends that impact global sourcing and supplier relationships

    ●    Collaborate with suppliers to improve quality, cost-effectiveness, and delivery performance.

    ●    Negotiate pricing, terms, and conditions with suppliers to achieve cost savings and favourable agreements

    ●   Conduct regular supplier performance reviews to ensure quality, delivery, and responsiveness meet company standards

    ●    Identify opportunities to consolidate suppliers, reduce lead times, and improve overall supply chain resilience

    ●    Manage the entire procurement lifecycle, including requisition processing, purchase order creation, and order tracking

    ●    Liaise with logistics team to ensure timely and accurate delivery of goods and services to meet operational demands

    ●    Liaise with the Sales Team on customer’s specific components requests and provide inventory information

    Job requirements

    ●    Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Supply Chain Management, Logistics or related field

    ●    Strong sense of urgency and attention to details

    ●    Bilingual is preferred

    ●    Proactive, excellent communication and negotiation skills

    ●    Analytical with a methodical approach to problem solving

    ●    Proficient in Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint

    ●    Candidates with no experience are welcome to apply!
